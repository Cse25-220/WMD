Known for his mumble-style delivery and prolific output, Future is credited with having pioneered the use of Auto-Tuned melodies in hip-hop and trap music.[10][11][12] Due to the sustained popularity of this musical style, he is commonly regarded as one of the most influential rappers of his generation.[13] Born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, Future signed a recording contract with Rocko's A1 Recordings in 2011, which entered a joint venture with Epic Records shortly after.
